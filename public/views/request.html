Project Request Form

Innovative Design provides graphic design and photography services for both campus and non-campus organizations.

Gold Tier does design work free-of-charge for UC Berkeley student organizations and events - exceptions apply for transporting photo equipment and fast deadlines. All project requests must be submitted three weeks in advance. To be considered a student group, the client must be listed in the UC Berkeley Student Organizations List found here: https://callink.berkeley.edu/Organizations.

Blue Tier handles non-campus affiliated along with any expedited project requests. Projects that need to be completed in two weeks or less are considered to be expedited requests. Price estimates for Blue Tier requests are given based on the timeline and size of the project.

Web Design Tier develops layout mockups and builds websites, while Video Tier composes and edits short promotional videos for local events (we do not provide video recording equipment or onsite filming). Requests that are not defined as a Gold Tier, Web Design Tier, or Video Tier project will be handled by Blue Tier.

Please keep in mind that Gold Tier projects (such as flyers) may be watermarked with Innovative Design's logo. Additionally, we ask that you refrain from tinkering with finished designs and/or claiming Innovative Design's work as your own.

The more information you can give us, the better we can serve you. We look forward to working with you.
